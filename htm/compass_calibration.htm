Compass Calibration

For best results with VideoRay CoPilot RI, the compass calibration should be completed to optimize its performance in your location. If you have moved 1 degree of Latitude or more from your last operating location or if you are operating near a large ferrous object like a ship, you should calibrate the compass. The compass calibration can be found in VideoRay Cockpit.
